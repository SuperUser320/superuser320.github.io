About Me

I'm a self-motivated, hardworking, computer engineering student at Michigan State University with a focus on radar signal processing, autonomous driving, robotics, and embedded electronics design.

When I'm not in class or studying I commit my time to personal projects, as featured below, and CANVAS SOAR; SOAR is a Student Organized Autonomy Research group at MSU primarily focused on competing in the three-year GM/SAE AutoDrive Challenge compeitition with the goal of developing a highly automated level 4 (SAE J3016) autonomous vehicle by 2020.

I also enjoy playing ultimate frisbee, ice hockey, riding single track mountain bike trails, and autocross/auto racing. In addition I pursue interests in photography and graphic design which can be found at Flickr and 500px.
